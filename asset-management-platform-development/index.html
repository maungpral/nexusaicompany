Our Asset Management Platform Development Services

At Blaize, we specialize in developing cutting-edge asset management platforms tailored to your specific needs. Our comprehensive services cover every aspect of digital asset management, ensuring your business operates efficiently and securely in the evolving financial landscape.

01

Asset Management Platform Development

Our modern, interoperable, and modular asset management solutions enable efficient operations across diverse assets, tokens, and derivatives. Leverage blockchain for immutable records, smart contracts for complex digital assets, and user-friendly decentralized applications for seamless onboarding and visual fund control.

02

Asset Visibility Solutions Development

Optimize your platform with asset visibility tools like dashboards, portfolio trackers, explorers, and price aggregators. Consolidate asset tracking into a single view, featuring historical balances, performance analytics, and risk assessments. Enjoy flexible search capabilities across all wallets, accounts, and platforms for streamlined financial oversight.

03

Wallet Solutions and Payment Providers

Access your portfolio through custom wallet solutions or integrate modern wallets. Easily transfer assets and manage your holdings with our custodial solutions, ensuring secure and efficient transactions.

04

Portfolio Management and Analysis Tools

Develop comprehensive tools for managing digital asset positions. Our services include visual trackers for current and historical P&L, price analysis, risk evaluation frameworks, and historical data aggregation, providing a robust portfolio management system.

05

Price Feeds and Oracles

We develop custom oracles and price feeds for accurate valuation and maintenance of tokenized assets, ensuring reliable and up-to-date pricing information. Tailored solutions for custom assets ensure precise assessments.

06

Trading Platforms

Create advanced asset exchange platforms with modern mechanisms like limit orders and leverage trading. Our solutions support seamless and secure trading experiences.

07

Derivatives and Complex Digital Assets

Develop platforms for derivatives and complex digital assets, including perpetual contracts. Our expertise in creating GMX-like protocols and dApp-chain platforms like Hyperliquid and dYdX offers comprehensive solutions for intricate DeFi instruments.

08

Synthetic Assets

Enhance your business with synthetic assets development. We create investment vehicles like pegged assets, decentralized indexes, and stablecoins. Our expertise enables the development of advanced synthetic instruments, redefining market strategies.

09

Audit

Secure your DeFi project with our thorough audit services. We identify vulnerabilities and optimize smart contract performance, ensuring your platform's integrity and trustworthiness. Rely on our expert audit team for comprehensive security.

Do you offer integration with popular DEXes and wallets?
Yes, our platforms feature robust integration capabilities with leading decentralized (and centralized) exchanges and wallets. This interoperability facilitates seamless asset transfers and consolidates portfolio management, enhancing user convenience and operational efficiency.
How do you ensure the security and privacy of users' digital assets on the management platforms you develop?
At Blaize, we prioritize security and privacy in our digital asset management platform development. We implement industry-leading encryption, multi-factor authentication, and cold storage solutions. Regular security audits and compliance with data protection regulations further ensure that users' assets and information remain secure and private.

Absolutely, our digital asset management platforms come equipped with advanced features such as real-time portfolio tracking, detailed analytics, and comprehensive reporting tools.
