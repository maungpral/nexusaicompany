Our Web3 Development Services

Harnessing over 6 years of blockchain expertise, Blaize introduces asset tokenization as a core component of its web3 development services. Eliminate the necessity to liquidate physical assets, allowing clients to unlock funds while retaining ownership. Embrace our turnkey solutions for streamlined asset management, covering development to post-launch oversight.

01

Smart Contract Implementation

Craft and deploy bespoke smart contracts – self-executing programs enforcing predefined rules on the blockchain, enabling secure and transparent automation of diverse business processes.

02

Decentralized Application (dApp) Development

Create and deploy custom decentralized applications (dApps) – autonomous programs running on blockchain networks with predefined rules. These self-executing applications revolutionize various business processes by providing secure and transparent automation.

03

NFT Minting and Marketplace

Produce NFTs for digital collectibles and collections, showcasing them on your customized NFT marketplace – whether custodial, non-custodial, niche, or universal.

04

Web3 Gaming Design

Create P2E and NFT-based multiplayer games, akin to renowned products like Axie Infinity and CryptoKitties. Implement a well-designed rewards system and a listed token for a comprehensive gaming experience.

05

DeFi Application Development

Explore the thriving DeFi sector within Web3, offering decentralized financial solutions for lending, borrowing, staking, and trading. Our services encompass centralized and decentralized exchanges, wallets, protocols, and other products unlocking blockchain potential.

06

Asset Tokenization and STO Services

Tokenize diverse assets and secure ownership on the blockchain. Our team ensures token security and conducts Security Token Offerings (STOs) for industries like real estate and natural resources.

07

Blockchain System Integration

Link existing systems with blockchain networks, enabling businesses to harness decentralized technology benefits while maintaining compatibility with current infrastructure.

08

NTT and Self-Sovereign Identity Solutions

Mint a non-transferable token (NTT) for user identity, and develop a Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) system. Build or integrate infrastructure for user identity authentication, ensuring robust security measures.
