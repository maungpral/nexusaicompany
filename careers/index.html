About Us

Founded in 2016, Blaize company covers all areas of blockchain and crypto development, including projects in DeFi, NFT-related products, blockchain ecosystems, protocols, and smart contracts. Blaize provides a wide range of services, including smart contract audits, custom blockchain development, tailor-made enterprise solutions, consulting, and much more.

We have completed over 70 successful projects in different areas of blockchain development and conducted more than 110 smart contract audits. Our clients are Aurora, 1inch, PeakDeFi, LeagueDAO, and Celo.
Our great team comprises over 65 professionals with the deepest expertise and world-class performance. We are sure that the key to success is a constant self-development and growth. And we do encourage it.

Why choose Blaize

Our values:

Supportive atmosphere

It is crucial for us that every employee feels comfortable with us. Therefore, we have mentoring programs, onboarding managers, and a clear KPI system with feedback. With us, you can count not only on support but also on financial assistance in difficult times.

Expert team

Our team and its expertise are our greatest strengths. Our employees have international certifications and create their own courses. To grow from junior to senior is a reality at Blaize.

Innovations

We operate on the technology edge already today. We deal with Polkadot and Cosmos ecosystems and already have mastered 20+ blockchains: Ethereum, NEAR, Solana, Arbitrum, and Optimism, naming a few.
While others learn, we already work with brand-new chains like Aptos, Sui, Secret Network, StarkNet. Blaize is a full-cycle blockchain development company, which helps us to provide the best solutions for your project.
