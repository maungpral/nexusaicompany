What are P2E Game and How Does it Actually Work?

P2E games are an innovative fusion of gaming and blockchain technology, allowing players to earn rewards by participating in various in-game activities. These rewards often come as cryptocurrencies or other digital assets that can be traded, sold, or used within the game ecosystem. 

Through blockchain technology, P2E games provide a transparent, secure, and decentralized environment for players to own and manage their in-game assets. P2E games have gained significant popularity in the gaming community by enabling players to monetize their gaming skills and time.

The underlying technology of P2E games involves using non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and other smart contracts on various blockchain ecosystems considering their features (bandwidth, transaction speed, scalability, etc.). NFTs represent unique digital assets, like in-game items or characters, and smart contracts govern the rules of the game, ensuring fairness and transparency.

Main Advantages of Play-to-Earn Games

Opportunity to Earn Money

One of the most significant benefits of P2E games is the potential for players to earn money while enjoying their gaming experience. By completing in-game tasks, participating in battles, or discovering valuable items, players can accumulate digital assets that can be traded, sold, or exchanged for real-world value. This financial incentive has attracted many players, who see P2E games as a way to monetize their skills and time spent gaming.

Creation of a Global Gaming Community

P2E games have fostered the growth of global gaming communities, bringing together players from different parts of the world to collaborate, compete, and trade in-game assets. This has led to the formation of vibrant and diverse ecosystems within P2E games, where players can forge new friendships, learn from each other, and build strong networks.

Enhanced Security for the Gaming Industry

By leveraging blockchain technology, P2E games offer a higher security level than traditional gaming platforms. Blockchain’s decentralized and transparent nature ensures that in-game assets are securely stored and easily verifiable, reducing the risk of fraud and hacking. Furthermore, the use of smart contracts ensures that transactions within the game are carried out fairly and according to predefined rules, providing players with a secure and trustworthy environment.

Democratization of Game Development

P2E games have also democratized the game development process by allowing smaller, independent developers to create successful games that can compete with established gaming giants. This has resulted in a more diverse gaming landscape, with innovative and unique titles developed by passionate teams from all around the globe.

Things to Consider When Developing a Play-to-Earn Game

The P2E game development process includes multiple stages and requires deep expertise combined with great performance to make success. Here’s the list of the most essential points to pay attention to when developing an NFT game project:

Sustainable and Scalable Business Model

A successful P2E game requires a sustainable and scalable business model that can support the game’s growth while maintaining its appeal to players. This includes devising a balanced reward system that encourages player engagement without causing inflation or undermining the game’s economy. Additionally, the game should have a clear roadmap for future updates and expansions to keep the player base engaged and foster long-term growth.

Elaborate Tokenomics Model

Tokenomics, the economic system surrounding a game’s native tokens or cryptocurrencies, is a crucial aspect of P2E game development. A well-designed tokenomics model should incentivize players to participate in the game and contribute to its ecosystem. This may include mechanisms for earning, spending, and staking tokens, as well as governance systems that allow players to have a say in the game’s development and direction.

Suitable Blockchain Platform

Selecting the right blockchain platform for your P2E game is essential, as blockchain properties such as transaction speed, security, and scalability will impact various aspects of the game. Popular blockchain platforms for P2E games include Ethereum, Solana, and Polygon, each offering unique advantages and trade-offs. Carefully consider factors such as network fees, transaction throughput, and developer community support when choosing a platform for your game.

Engaging Gameplay and Story

While the play-to-earn aspect is undoubtedly a significant draw for players, a P2E game should offer engaging gameplay and an immersive storyline in the first place to keep players invested in the long term. Striking the right balance between fun gameplay and profitable opportunities is critical to creating a successful P2E game.

Key Features of P2E Games

Fraud Detection and Prevention

To maintain a secure and fair gaming environment, P2E games must incorporate robust fraud prevention mechanisms. This may include monitoring for suspicious activities, implementing anti-bot measures for player accounts, and utilizing blockchain’s inherent security features to protect in-game assets.

Tokenomics

An essential feature of P2E games is a well-designed tokenomics model that governs the game’s native tokens or game currency. This includes token distribution, utility, and mechanisms for earning, spending, and staking tokens within the game. A successful tokenomics model should incentivize players to participate in the game and contribute to its ecosystem while maintaining a healthy in-game economy.

Micro Payments

To facilitate seamless in-game transactions, P2E games should support micro payments, allowing players to make small, frequent transactions without incurring high fees. This enables players to easily trade and sell their in-game assets, enhancing the overall gaming experience and fostering a thriving in-game economy.

In-Game Currency

A crucial component of P2E games is the implementation of an in-game currency that can be earned, traded, and spent within the game ecosystem. This currency should have real-world value, enabling players to monetize their gaming efforts and providing a tangible incentive to participate in the game. In many P2E games, in-game currency takes the form of tokens, bonds or other cryptoassets built on blockchain platforms.

Player-Owned Assets and NFTs

P2E games should incorporate player-owned assets, often represented as non-fungible tokens (NFTs), to empower players with true ownership and control over their in-game items, characters, and other digital assets. NFTs, which are unique and indivisible, enable players to trade, sell, or use their assets within the game ecosystem, fostering a vibrant and dynamic in-game economy.

P2E Game Architecture

Developing a P2E game requires a well-designed architecture that seamlessly integrates gaming components with blockchain technology. P2E game developers usually need to have wide expertise in blockchain as well as programming. Here’s a high-level overview of the key elements that make up a typical P2E game architecture:

dApp (Decentralized Application)

A dApp provides a transparent, secure, and trustless environment for users. In the context of P2E games, dApps enable seamless management of in-game assets, rewards, and transactions, elevating the overall gaming experience. DApps grant users access to smart contracts, used by the games.

Smart Contracts

Smart contracts are self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. In nft games, smart contracts govern various aspects of the game, such as asset ownership, token distribution, and in-game transactions. In P2E games smart contracts play a key role in games’ mechanics: they provide gaming logic, and ensure transparency, fairness, and security in their operations.

Blockchain Ecosystem

The blockchain ecosystem picked for a separate P2E game grants various features and opportunities for a project. Some chains provide extra scalability and extra rapid transactions, which is crucial for a popular game. Some of the ecosystems are aimed at nft projects and support additional infrastructure for them (like marketplaces, special decentralized storage services, aggregators, etc). Choosing the suitable blockchain depends greatly on a separate project’s game mechanics and goals.

Decentralized Storage

Decentralized storage solutions, such as IPFS or Filecoin, can be used to store game assets, metadata, and other data securely and transparently. This ensures that in-game assets are permanently accessible and not subject to the control of any single entity.

Decentralized Marketplace

A decentralized marketplace allows players to trade, sell, and purchase in-game assets, such as NFTs, tokens, or items, in a peer-to-peer manner. These marketplaces can be built on top of existing decentralized exchange (DEX) protocols or developed as custom solutions for the specific game.
