Blaize and GetBlock: An Essential Partnership for Development Expertise

Sergey Onyshchenko
2 years ago

The landscape of decentralized applications is one that’s constantly evolving, driven by innovation, need, and collaboration. As pioneers in the space, Blaize and GetBlock have individually played pivotal roles in defining the standards for excellence. Today, we’re elated to announce that the two are now joining forces!

Why GetBlock is Essential in the World of dApps

Before delving into the partnership specifics, it’s paramount to understand the value proposition that GetBlock brings. GetBlock provides superior node infrastructure essential for building and operating dApps.

To paint a clearer picture, think of a node as a relay and gateway – it’s the communication channel between the decentralized network and the applications built on top. Having reliable, fast, and scalable nodes is crucial; this is where GetBlock shines brightest. Their multi-blockchain support, spanning Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB Smart Chain, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and many more, gives developers expansive room to maneuver and design.

Top Reasons to Choose GetBlock as Your Node Service Provider

  • Whether you’re a novice just dipping your toes into blockchain or a seasoned developer, GetBlock offers rapid personal consulting to guide you every step of the way.
  • Their comprehensive library of technical guides, node endpoints & methods, API references, and much more ensures developers have all the tools they need to get their projects off the ground.
  • Custom SLAs. GetBlock isn’t one-size-fits-all. They offer fully featured Service Level Agreements tailored to your project’s performance demands and business model.

Blaize and GetBlock: Synergizing Expertise

By integrating GetBlock into Blaize’s technology stack, we’re augmenting our offering for our clients. This partnership means not only do our clients get premier blockchain development and consultancy, but they also have direct access to top-tier node infrastructure. It’s like having the best of both worlds under one roof!

An alternative to previous-gen node providers like Infura and Alchemy, GetBlock stands as a beacon of reliability and scalability. With Blaize’s reputation in blockchain development, dApp creators are assured of a streamlined, efficient, and robust ecosystem to bring their visions to life. Also, our Web3 Security team gets an excellent instrument to enhance auditing techniques during integration testing stages for verification of 3rd parties integrations. And vice-versa – Blaize Security team is first in the line to provide extensive consulting on security enhancements for the GetBlock users.
