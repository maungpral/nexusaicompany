Our Cross-chain Development Services

Explore the full spectrum of cross-chain development services by Blaize. Aligned with industry standards, our custom solutions ensure scalable interoperability, robust security, and technical excellence across selected blockchain ecosystems, catering to our clients’ most sophisticated needs.

01

Cross-chain Liquidity Management Solutions

Optimize liquidity management across several chains with our cross-chain solutions. We focus on speed, security, and targeted interoperability for efficient cross-chain interactions. Our team will help you with integration with LayerZero or any other cross-chain solutions - or develop a custom one for you.

02

Bridge Development

Connect multiple blockchains simultaneously with our multichain bridge solutions. These bridges expand interoperability and liquidity, perfect for enterprises engaging with various blockchain networks. Either it is a chain-to-chain bridge, or a connection with multiple chains, governed or trustless - our team will develop a cross-chain solution based on all of your requirements.

03

Cross-Chain Messaging Layer Development

Facilitate seamless communication and asset exchanges with our cross-chain messaging and liquidity layer services. This unified layer supports data transfer and smart contract interactions across diverse platforms, enhancing decentralized application functionality. Our team will help with integration with Wormhole, Axelar or any other cross-chain messaging solutions.

04

Interchain Infrastructure

Our team will build a custom connectivity infrastructure for your blockchain solution. Either it is a state channel, a custom ledger of validators or any other solution to work with outcoming requests - Blaize team will develop the interchain connectivity layer and integrate it with target chains of your choice.

05

Interoperable Solutions Development

Integrate your protocol into modern interoperable blockchain networks. Our team are experts in parachain development for Polkadot ecosystem and in development of IBC-compatibles solutions for Cosmos ecosystem. Connect your platform with dozens of blockchains from Polkadot or Cosmos networks.

06

Bridge Audit

Ensure the integrity and security of your cross-chain solutions with our comprehensive audit services. We specialize in identifying vulnerabilities and optimizing performance, providing detailed assessments to enhance the safety and efficiency of your bridge solutions. Trust our expert team to maintain the highest standards of security for your blockchain connections.

Benefits of Cross-chain Development

Enable Cross-Chain Connectivity

Facilitate seamless transactions and interactions between different blockchain networks, expanding your project’s reach and utility by connecting isolated blockchains, which can significantly enhance user experience and asset accessibility

Liquidity Opportunities

Unlock new horizons for liquidity usage. Our cross-chain development enables efficient asset transfers across various blockchain networks, providing access to a wider range of DeFi ecosystems. This allows users to optimize liquidity, diversify investments, and enhance financial strategies across multiple platforms

Independence and Interoperability

Achieve greater autonomy by not relying on single blockchain solutions. Our cross-chain solutions enhance interoperability, allowing your project to communicate and transact across multiple blockchains, fostering a more resilient and flexible ecosystem

Improve Scalability

Address scalability issues inherent in single networks by distributing transactions and workloads across multiple chains. This not only speeds up processing times but also significantly reduces congestion and associated costs on any one blockchain

Enhanced Security Protocols

Benefit from advanced security measures integrated into cross-chain development to protect against potential threats and vulnerabilities. These protocols ensure the safe transfer of assets and data, building trust and reliability in your cross-chain services

Diversified Ecosystem Participation

Participate in a broader range of blockchain ecosystems, accessing diverse dApps and services. This inclusivity boosts your project's visibility and user engagement, contributing to a more vibrant and dynamic community

FAQ

What are the key features and functionalities you offer in cross-chain development?
At Blaize, we specialize in developing cross-chain solutions that enhance interoperability and facilitate seamless transactions between disparate blockchain networks. Our solutions feature assets porting and swapping, liquidity pooling, and data messaging, all built with high transaction throughput and minimal latency to ensure a smooth user experience.
Which blockchain networks and protocols do you support for cross-chain solution integration?
We support a wide range of blockchain networks and protocols, including all EVM compatible networks, Solana, Sui, Aptos, TON and many others. Our team is adept at working with various blockchain technologies to ensure comprehensive cross-chain ecosystem connectivity.
How do you ensure the security and reliability of the cross-chain solutions you develop?
Security and reliability are paramount at Blaize. We implement rigorous testing and auditing processes, including smart contract audits, penetration testing, and continuous monitoring, to ensure that our cross-chain solutions are secure and function reliably under various network conditions.
Can you provide examples of successful cross-chain solutions projects you have completed?
Blaize has successfully implemented several notable projects including some public cases as the automated transaction tracking and verification service for Remme Chain, the smart contract security audit for the Rainbow Bridge by Aurora, and the comprehensive audit of Kale Bridge smart contracts. These projects showcase our expertise in ensuring secure and efficient cross-chain bridge functionality.
What is your development process for building custom cross-chain solutions?
Our development process begins with a detailed analysis of your specific needs, followed by the design of a customized solution. We then move to the development and testing phases, ensuring each component integrates flawlessly with existing systems. Finally, we provide deployment support and ongoing maintenance to ensure long-term success.
How do you handle the challenges associated with cross-chain interoperability and scalability?
Blaize addresses these challenges by using scalable architecture and optimizing the cross-chain solutions for low latency and high throughput. We also continuously update our technologies and strategies to adapt to new developments in blockchain technology, ensuring robust interoperability and scalability.
What are the pricing models and timelines for your cross-chain development services?
We offer flexible pricing models tailored to the scope and complexity of your project, including fixed-price and time-and-materials options. The timeline for development varies based on the project's requirements but typically ranges from a few weeks to several months. We ensure transparency and predictability in both pricing and scheduling to meet your budgeting and deployment needs.
